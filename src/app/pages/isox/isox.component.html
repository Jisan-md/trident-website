iSOX– Intelligent Sales Order Xpress

With the recent quantum leap in technology, the organizations have speed up the work to be lean and efficient. However this has poised bigger challenges to keep the data clean, of high quality and automated. Our Innovation lab consolidated few success stories across clients and integrated those with SAP technologies powered by SAP BTP® that resulted in iSOX (Intelligent Sales Order Xpress).

Intelligent Sales Order Xpress (iSOX) provides end-to-end data services around Sales Order Maintenance.

Transform your Sales Order Management & Maintenance:

Intelligent Sales Order Xpress at iDMX gives enterprises full transparency into sales order maintenance activities and complete control over sales order data across the channels and execution process. ISOX offers a pre-validation stage for the Sales Orders’ Data called Sales Order Requests (SOR) that allows validation of the data, identification of the data issues and user maintenance before sales orders are released to SAP® or Core ERP. Sales Orders are managed by exception and the history logs provide Artificial Intelligence for error management and recommendations.

The application is available as an on-premise solution or based on the SAP Cloud Platform – depending on your needs.

Features:

    Visibility of Sales Orders Data at the entry point.

    Real-time Automated Sales Order Data Validation with SAP or core ERP.

    Easier Data maintenance without locking and system performance issues.

    Clean data sent to SAP for streamlined processing.

    Improved Accuracy & Efficiency in operations.

    Powerful Operational and Executive Dashboards and Reporting.

    Scalable Workflow for multiple hierarchies.

    Role Based Authorization to View/Edit/Reject SO Data.

    Seamless Internal User Experience.

    Least Customer Service training is required.

    Simple and cost effective Integration.

How Does it Work:

ISOX is a Cloud based Application based on SAP BTP technology that is used to stage the Sales Order data in Cloud Data Base for data validation and error handling before sending to SAP S/4/ERP for the creation of the Sales Document.

A Front-End application is provided to the users to upload the sales order data, display the inbound data, validate the data using APIs and maintain/update the required data before it is re-validated and finally sent to SAP for Sales Order creation.
