Introduction

Welcome to a new era of SAP Master Data Management with Intelligent Data Management Express (iDMX), McKinsol's cutting-edge proprietary tool. iDMX is designed to revolutionize the management of SAP Master data across various domains, including Article Master, Vendor Master, Customer Master, Material Master, BOM, Routing, Cost Center, Profit Center, and GL Account. With iDMX, you can effortlessly handle CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, and Delete) for SAP Master data, whether it's single entries or mass data operations.
